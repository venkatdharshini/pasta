The Special Egg Pasta

This is a food that is very tasty at any time. Did you guess it? Yes it is Pasta. We can make many different types of Pasta. And also it is in spicy version and sweet version. Both of them are awesome. Some pastas are White sauce pasta, Red sauce pasta, Veg pasta, Egg pasta and many more. But, Now I am going to show how to make egg pasta.

Ingredients:(For one person)

  1. 200 mg of pasta(Any type)
  2. Water(which mentioned in packet)
  3. 2 Eggs
  4. spices
  5. Tomato sauce
  6. Soya sauce
  7. Ginger and Garlic(optional)

Procedure

First take pasta add oil, And boil it. After boiled a pasta, take one kunda put it on the stove. and add some oil. add some masala to it. you can also add some biriyani leaf for flavour. Next add eggs and satuae them evenly.add boiled pasta. mix well. add some tomato and chilli sauce. Now, serve it with nice decoration, Enjoy your pasta
